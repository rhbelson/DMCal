About NUDM 2019
More about CIS of Chicago?
  • Communities In Schools of Chicago is a nonprofit dedicated to addressing the opportunity gap in education by ensuring that every Chicago public school student graduates high school prepared for success. CIS of Chicago implements programs that fall within four core areas: college and career readiness; the arts; health and wellness; and behavioral and mental health.
When do I get fed?
  • You get free food all 30 hours and we can ensure you will never be hungry! You will get a snack twice each 3-hour block, and breakfast, lunch, and dinner on Saturday.
 How many people in the tent?
  • Over 1000 students, so approximately 1 in 5 Northwestern students participate in this campus tradition
